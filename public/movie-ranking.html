The best movies according to Carolina

My top three movies of all time

Sense and Sensibility (1995)

Directed by Ang Lee and played by Alan Rickman, Emma Thompson, Kate Winslet and many more brilliant english actors.
It's based on the homonymous novel of Jane Austen, one of my favourites.

Troy (2004)

This one directed by Wolfgang Petersen, played by Brad Pitt as Achilles and Eric Bana as Hector from the epic poem of Homer, the Illiad, that I'm fond of.

The Godfather (1972)

Directed by Francis Coppola, displays the story of a powerful clan of sicilian descent, the Corleone's family, the cornerstone of the mafia in New York.
